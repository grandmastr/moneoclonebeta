About Blockchain Freelancer



Blockchain Freelancer

Hire hand-picked blockchain freelancers. Blockchain Freelancer is a platform to run large projects or book one-off gigs; all with secure bitcoin escrow payments.


What we do

Whether you're a startup or institution; hire qualified and vetted blockchain freelancers. Quality is everything; only the top 5% pass Blockchain Freelancer's strict quality checks. The Blockchain Freelancer team is always ready to help resource your project and support you were needed.

Blockchain Freelancer is the place to consult for Bitcoin; designers, marketers, developers, and consultancies; access more clients, build trust, and showcase your skills. Blockchain Freelancer deduct 10% from completed work to run and develop the site, and pay for marketing. Everyone benefits from straightforward payments with Bitcoin.


Product

Blockchain Freelancer is a combined effort of thousands of hours of work, from frontend and backend developers, marketers, growth hackers, product managers, UI designers, blockchain industry experts, and researchers over 18 months.

Blockchain Freelancer is a powerful, yet simple product. The complexity of the backend workflows, marketing integrations, and the payment system is transparent to Blockchain Freelancer's customers. Payments are all localised, so prices are displayed in local currencies.

Blockchain Freelancer support hourly jobs, and fixed price jobs and gigs. Whatever path a client takes to working with a freelancer; Blockchain Freelancer's comprehensive milestone, invoicing, and escrow system supports projects of any length and budget.

Freelancers can create a customisable (and beautiful) profile to showcase their skills and sell services; add custom banners and logos, upload photos and videos, create gigs with discounts, verify identity and qualifications, and a lot more.


Difference

Bitcoin is ushering in the next generation of commerce, and blockchain is changing industries. While there are numerous gig and freelance sites around, most of them are flooded with low-quality services and bargain-basement discounts.

Blockchain Freelancer is different. Whether you're running an ICO, or an enterprise building a blockchain solution; Blockchain Freelancer is a platform to resource and run your projects. Our team will work one-on-one with you.


Mission

The team behind Blockchain Freelancer has a goal of building a simple and trustworthy platform. Bitcoin and blockchain technology is changing the world, and we are passionate about contributing to the community. We aspire to be the go-to place for sourcing blockchain talent.

Escoin

Escoin (Escrow Coin) is a separate brand and service owned and operated by Blockchain Freelancer Services Ltd. Escoin is a secure payment system providing Bitcoin payment, key storage, and settlement services, external to the Blockchain Freelancer platform.
